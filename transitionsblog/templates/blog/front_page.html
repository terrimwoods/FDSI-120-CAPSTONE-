I was born a twin in Camden, New Jersey to Allen and Thomasinia Woods. I was the seventh out of nine children born to my parents. dis parturient montes My father was moving up the ranks in the United States Navy so we moved around quite a bit giving me a desire to travel the world and experience new things.

Growing up I was pretty quiet, good in school and average in sports. I enjoyed spending time with my family, mainly my twin and my mother who loved to cook and bake sweets. I loved that on my birthday she would bake my favorite cake and allow me to have it all to myself. Growing up with eight siblings in the house made this little generosity a heavenly delight. You have no idea!! Lies, I am a world class athlete. In my world.

I had separation anxiety elementary school was hard. Obviously I didn't write this particular article.I went to community college, I hate school with a passion.

Kindergarten was my worst year of school ever! Good thing that things got better as I mastered the levels of scholastics and graduated with honors.... well maybe not with honors but I did graduate and went on to college. I didn't really like college but I knew I needed it to elevate to where I wanted to be in life.

More BS from my sister

After finishing up a stint in college I entered into the United States Air Force where life opened up for me.

My Travels.....

The United States Airforce gave me the opportunity to travel while I work......

She knows nothing of my travels or sacrifices

Retiring from the Airforce was bittersweet. I made so many friends and colleagues that I miss but am so happy to be back in my own space doing what I like to do best, sleeping in, moving around town with friends, of course this was pre covid-19 and just doing nothing

Retirement is Awesome

Just doing nothing can get boring, just kidding! it is so nice to do nothing! However I did decide to take some online classes that brings me to this project right here. It has been challenging but insightful and sometimes ... what's the word I am looking for ... procrastination makes it almost imposible to search for things.

  • Haiti for Humanitarian.
  • Qatar for security
  • Afghanistan for Operation Enduring Freedom.
  • Turkey, I got to live there for 15 months.

State Side

  2. Elmondorf AFB Anchorage, Alaska; I learned to drive in the snow.
  3. Barksdale AFB Shreveport, Louisiana; humidity will reck you.
